Key findings

What is costing the most, in order.

Website · Critical

The site is down, and the last version was a placeholder template

The domain currently fails to load, and the most recent archived version was a generic personal-trainer template: lorem-ipsum body copy, an empty store, and fake testimonials ("J. Backlovley," "Amelia Joe") over counters reading "0 Years / 0 Clients." For a 156K brand, the destination is a broken or abandoned page, so nearly all that attention has nowhere real to land.

Fix: a proper conversion site with real copy, real proof, and a working funnel, replacing the dead template.
Monetisation · Critical

The whole engine runs by hand

Acquisition, the sales conversation, and delivery all appear to run manually, one person at a time, which is capped by your own hours. Whatever you offer, there is no self-serve funnel that lets it scale or sell while you sleep. A movement audience this size can support a productized program, a membership or a challenge running on autopilot. That is the single largest revenue gap.

Fix: productize the offer into a self-serve funnel (landing page, checkout, automated delivery) so income stops being capped by your calendar.
Owned audience · Critical

No email list, so you rent your audience

With no real capture, 156K followers stay on a platform you do not own. One algorithm change or account issue and the reach is gone. There is also no way to launch a product to warm buyers on demand.

Fix: a free lead magnet (a mobility or movement mini-course) to convert followers into owned email subscribers.
Proof · High

Fake placeholder testimonials undercut real credibility

The site's results section was never finished: the testimonials are template placeholders with invented names and the stat block reads "0 Clients." Your actual credibility lives in the feed, but the one place a buyer checks at the decision moment actively works against you.

Fix: a real results and transformation section with genuine client stories, replacing every placeholder.
The one-line takeaway

"You have a 156K movement audience pointing at a website that will not load, and a funnel you run entirely by hand. Put a real site, a productized self-serve offer and an email capture behind that reach and the same attention starts compounding instead of leaking."
