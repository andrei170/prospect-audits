Questions

Good to know.

Do I need any equipment?

No. The program is built around your own body. A little floor space is all you need.

I am not flexible or fit. Can I still do this?

Yes. Every movement starts from where you are and progresses gently. It is designed for real, stiff, everyday bodies.

How much time does it take?

Short, focused sessions you can fit into a busy day. Consistency matters more than length.

How long do I have access?

Lifetime. Move through it at your own pace and come back to it whenever you like.
