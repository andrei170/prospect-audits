Honest fit check

Is The Primal Method for you?

This is for you if

  • You want to feel strong and mobile in everyday life, not just under a bar
  • You prefer following a clear plan over piecing clips together
  • You like training with little or no equipment, at home or outdoors
  • You are ready to practice a few times a week and stay consistent

This is not for you if

  • You want a quick fix with zero effort or practice
  • You are only chasing max numbers and nothing else
  • You would rather have every session hand-fed one at a time by DM
  • You are not willing to start where your body is today

Good to know

Questions, answered

Do I need any equipment or a gym?
No. The Primal Method is built around bodyweight and floor-based movement. A little open space is all you need, at home or outdoors.
I am not flexible or strong. Can I still start?
Yes. Every module starts from the ground up with regressions, so you begin exactly where your body is today and build from there.
How much time does it take each week?
The practice plans are designed for a few short sessions a week. It is self-paced, so you fit it around your life rather than the other way around.
Is this a live program or self-paced?
The core program is fully self-paced and available the moment you join. The Guided and 1:1 tiers add live check-ins and coaching on top.
What if it is not right for me?
You have 14 days to try the program. If it is not a fit, reach out and we will make it right. Details are in the reassurance section above.
